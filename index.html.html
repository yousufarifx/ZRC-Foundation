Our Mission

Transforming Zindagis with Education, Empowerment & Hope

ZRC Foundation (Zindagi ke Rang Charitable Foundation) is a youth-led nonprofit transforming lives in underserved communities across Pakistan. We focus on providing free education, empowering youth and women, organizing sports and creative activities for children, and improving community wellbeing through practical initiatives like water dispensers and awareness drives..

Our volunteers work tirelessly to provide free educational resources, mentorship programs, and community development initiatives that create sustainable change.

Our Story

ZRC Foundation began when Yousuf Arif, at 17, started tutoring three underprivileged children at home. Inspired by this small act, he envisioned a nonprofit that would go beyond education‚Äîsupporting community wellbeing through food, clothing, healthcare, and more.

Today, ZRC is a youth-led movement working to uplift lives across Pakistan through compassion, education, and service.

Founder & Chairperson

Yousuf is an 18-year-old A-Level student, entrepreneur, and youth changemaker who founded ZRC Foundation with a vision to uplift underserved communities across Pakistan.
